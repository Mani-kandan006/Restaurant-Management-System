Our Story

Warm, authentic, and passionate — this is Mani's promise.

From a humble kitchen to your table

Mani's Kitchen began with a love for simple, honest food. Founded by Mani Kandan in 2018, we started as a neighborhood eatery focusing on traditional South Indian flavors. Over the years, we expanded our menu to include dishes from across India and around the world while staying true to fresh ingredients and hand-made spices.
